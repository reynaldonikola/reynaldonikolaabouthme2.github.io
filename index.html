My personal interests

I enjoy programming because it feels like solving puzzles that can actually help people. I like learning how different parts of a computer work together, and I especially like building small projects and seeing them run. When something finally works after debugging, it’s a great feeling.

Another interest of mine is motorcycles. I like the focus and responsibility that comes with riding, and I also enjoy learning about maintenance and safety. Riding clears my head and gives me a break from screens and stress, especially after long work shifts or a heavy week of classes.

I also love spending time with my family and playing video games when I have free time. Family keeps me grounded and motivates me to keep improving myself. Games are fun, but they also remind me how creativity and technology can work together to build amazing experiences.
